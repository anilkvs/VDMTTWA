Passion for the Game

At VDMTTWA, we believe that the spirit of Table Tennis thrives regardless of age. Our Masters players continue to showcase dedication, discipline, and excitement every time they step onto the court. Whether it’s a friendly rally or a competitive match, their love for the game inspires players across all generations. We aim to build a vibrant community where experience meets enthusiasm, and where every spin, serve, and smash is celebrated with pride.

Our Events

Our calendar is filled with exciting, well-organized events that bring together skilled Masters from across the region. From district-level ranking tournaments to friendly matches and workshops, each event is designed to promote healthy competition and team spirit. These events offer players a platform to showcase their talent, connect with peers, and earn recognition through a transparent point-based ranking system. Every match played is a celebration of sportsmanship and dedication to the game.

Growing Together

VDMTTWA is more than just a table tennis association — it’s a growing family of passionate players, supporters, and volunteers. We believe in the power of shared experiences, mentorship, and mutual respect to elevate the game. Through regular practice, spirited competition, and community-building events, we foster a positive environment where every player feels valued and inspired. Together, we are creating a lasting legacy for Table Tennis in our region, one rally at a time.

ABOUT VDMTTWA

VDMTTWA is a community of passionate master table tennis players from Visakhapatnam, focused on organizing events, maintaining rankings, and supporting players above a certain age group.

Meet the dedicated individuals behind the association who work tirelessly to bring table tennis to the forefront for masters in the district.
